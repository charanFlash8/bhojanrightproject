Our Mission

BhojanRight is India's first real-time surplus food rights network that bridges the gap between food waste and hunger through technology and community action.

The Big Problem

Every day in India, thousands of kilos of edible food are wasted by hotels, weddings, hostels, and homes—while millions go to bed hungry, including children, elderly, and migrants.

Our Solution

We leverage data, community networks, and legal frameworks to treat food access as a right, not a charity. Our model ensures food safety, dignity, and accountability while transforming food waste into a rights-based solution.

How It Works

  • App + Website: Food donors report surplus instantly. Volunteers and mapped beneficiaries are alerted in real-time via SMS or WhatsApp.
  • Hunger Ambassadors: Our trained volunteers map hunger hotspots and build local compassion ecosystems.
  • Legal & Policy Advocacy: We fight for a "Right to Surplus Food," food waste laws, tax benefits, and public logistics support.
  • BhojanBoxes: Smart, monitored community food fridges in every district.
