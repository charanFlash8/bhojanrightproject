Meet Our Team

Charan Banda

Founder and CEO

is the visionary CEO and founder of BhojanRight. At just 17 years old, Charan is a high school student with a bold mission: to end food injustice and build a compassionate society. Driven by empathy and innovation, he founded BhojanRight to give surplus food a purpose and make hunger a thing of the past. Charan is not only a social change-maker but also a passionate space and AI enthusiast. He believes in merging technology with humanity to solve real-world problems and build a future where basic needs are met for all. His leadership reflects youthful energy, bold ideas, and a deep commitment to fairness and dignity.

Mohammad Umar Moinuddin

Co-founder

Co-founder of BhojanRight, Umar Moinuddin is also Charan Banda's Bestfriend and patner. Committed to the same vision of eliminating food injustice, Umar has cooperated with Charan to turn their dream into reality. A tech-savvy as well as an avid learner, he believes in harnessing innovation to make the world more just and free from hunger. With his commitment, collaboration, and determination, he is an indispensable asset to BhojanRight's journey.

Ayan Ali

Master

Ayan Ali is a key contributor to BhojanRight's development, helping to build the foundational ideas and strategies that power our mission. As a close friend of both the founder and co-founder, Ayan brings unique perspectives and technical expertise to our team.
